About the bot

SentinelOne is a powerful, feature-rich moderation bot designed to help you maintain order and enforce rules within your Discord server. Built with a focus on ease of use and effectiveness, SentinelOne Bot ensures that your community remains safe, respectful, and enjoyable for everyone.

Key Features:

  • Rule Configuration: Set up custom rules for your server. Whether it's offensive language, spamming, or any other unwanted behavior, you decide what’s not allowed.
  • Violation Thresholds: Define how many violations trigger specific actions, such as mutes, or bans.
  • Cumulative Points System: A core feature of the SentinelOne bot designed to ensure fair and effective moderation by tracking user behavior over time. This system assigns points to each infraction based on its severity, and cumulative points help in determining appropriate disciplinary actions.

Detailed Logging:

  • Activity Logs: Keep track of all moderation actions taken by the bot. Easily review who was muted, banned, and the reasons for these actions.
  • Infraction History: Access detailed records of individual users' infractions, helping you make informed decisions about further actions.

User-Friendly Interface:

  • Commands: Simple and intuitive slash commands for managing the bot. Easily configure settings and review logs with straightforward commands
  • Role Management: Seamlessly integrates with your server’s role system to assign appropriate permissions and restrictions.

Why Choose SentinelOne Bot?

  • Reliability: Our bot is built to handle even the busiest servers, ensuring that no infraction goes unnoticed.
  • Customization: Tailor the bot’s behavior to fit your server’s unique needs and community guidelines.
  • Support: Get access to our dedicated support team for any questions or issues you might encounter.
