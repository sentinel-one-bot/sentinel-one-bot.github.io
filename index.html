About the Bot

SentinelOne is a moderation bot designed to meet all your moderation needs. You can set up custom reasons and points and punish accordingly. You can also set for how long each mute should last based on the points. Everything in the bot is dynamic and nothing is fixed. This makes it very unique as compared to other moderation bots as they focus on keeping a fixed moderation base.

Features

  • Customizable reasons and points
  • Customizable mute time based on points
  • Storing additional info to each punishment based on the unique punishment ID
  • Ban polls
